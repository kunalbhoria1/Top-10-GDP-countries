Italy

  • Geography of Italy

    Italy, located in Southern Europe, is a country with a diverse geography that includes rugged mountains, fertile plains, and a long Mediterranean coastline. It is shaped like a boot and is known for its iconic landmarks, historical sites, and beautiful landscapes. Italy is home to famous cities such as Rome, Venice, Florence, and Milan, each with its own unique charm.
