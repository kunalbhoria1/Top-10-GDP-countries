Canada

  • Geography of Canada

    Canada, located in North America, is the second-largest country in the world by land area. It spans from the Atlantic Ocean in the east to the Pacific Ocean in the west, and it shares a long border with the United States to the south. Canada's geography is incredibly diverse, featuring vast forests, rugged mountains (such as the Rocky Mountains), expansive prairies, and numerous lakes and rivers. The country also boasts stunning coastlines along the Atlantic, Pacific, and Arctic Oceans.
