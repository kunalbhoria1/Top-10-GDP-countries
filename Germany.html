Germany

  • Geography of Germany

    Germany, located in central Europe, is known for its diverse geography that includes forests, rivers, mountains, and coastal areas along the North Sea and Baltic Sea. The country shares borders with nine other European countries, including France, Austria, and Poland. Germany's landscape is marked by the Rhine and Danube rivers, the Bavarian Alps, the Black Forest, and picturesque towns along the Romantic Road.
