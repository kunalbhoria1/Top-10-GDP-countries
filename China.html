China

  • Geography of the China

    China, the world's most populous country, boasts a diverse geography spanning vast landscapes. It is situated in East Asia and shares borders with 14 other countries, including Russia, India, and Vietnam. China features a varied terrain, including mountain ranges such as the Himalayas and Kunlun Mountains, vast deserts like the Gobi, fertile plains like the North China Plain, and scenic rivers such as the Yangtze and Yellow River.
