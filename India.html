India

  • Geography of India

    India, located in South Asia, is a vast and diverse country with varied geography. It is bordered by the Indian Ocean to the south, the Arabian Sea to the southwest, and the Bay of Bengal to the southeast. India is characterized by the majestic Himalayas in the north, the fertile plains of the Ganges and Yamuna rivers, expansive deserts like the Thar Desert in the west, and lush tropical forests in the south.
