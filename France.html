France

  • Geography of France

    France, located in Western Europe, is known for its diverse geography, ranging from the snow-capped Alps in the east to the sandy beaches of the French Riviera in the south. The country is bordered by Belgium, Luxembourg, Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Monaco, Spain, and Andorra. It boasts picturesque countryside, vineyards, rolling hills, and iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Loire Valley, and the French Alps.
