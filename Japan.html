Japan

  • Geography of Japan

    Japan is an island nation located in East Asia, consisting of four main islands: Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku, along with numerous smaller islands. The country is known for its diverse geography, including mountainous regions, volcanic landscapes, and coastal plains. Iconic landmarks such as Mount Fuji, Japan's highest peak, and the stunning cherry blossom trees are synonymous with Japan's natural beauty.
