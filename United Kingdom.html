United Kingdom

  • Geography of the United Kingdom

    The United Kingdom (UK) is an island nation located off the northwestern coast of mainland Europe. It consists of four constituent countries: England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. The UK is surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean to the west and the North Sea to the east. It boasts diverse landscapes, including rolling hills, rugged coastlines, fertile plains, and mountains such as the Scottish Highlands and the Snowdonia range in Wales.
