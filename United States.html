  • Geography of the United States of America

    The United States of America, located in North America, is the third-largest country in the world by land area. It is bordered by Canada to the north, Mexico to the south, the Atlantic Ocean to the east, and the Pacific Ocean to the west. The country encompasses a wide range of geographical features, including vast plains, rugged mountains, deserts, and forests. Notable landmarks include the Rocky Mountains, the Grand Canyon, the Great Lakes, and the Mississippi River.
